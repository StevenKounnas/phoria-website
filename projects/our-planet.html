Client Netflix / Silverback Films
Role XR Production & Development
Year 2024
Virtual Reality Augmented Reality Conservation Educational

Overview

PHORIA partnered with Netflix and Silverback Films to create an immersive companion experience for the groundbreaking documentary series "Our Planet". This project combines cutting-edge VR and AR technology with powerful conservation storytelling to bring viewers closer to nature than ever before.

Working alongside WWF (World Wildlife Fund), we developed interactive experiences that allow users to explore endangered ecosystems, encounter wildlife in their natural habitats, and understand the urgent need for environmental action.

The Challenge

Create an immersive experience that complements Netflix's flagship nature documentary while:

  • Maintaining scientific accuracy and educational integrity
  • Supporting multiple platforms (VR headsets, mobile AR)
  • Conveying the urgency of conservation without overwhelming viewers
  • Integrating seamlessly with Netflix's existing content ecosystem

Our Solution

We developed a multi-platform XR experience featuring:

Virtual Reality Component: Fully immersive VR environments for Meta Quest and other headsets, allowing users to stand in the middle of rainforests, oceans, and frozen tundras. Experience wildlife encounters that would be impossible in real life.

Augmented Reality Features: Mobile AR experiences that bring Our Planet's ecosystems into your living room. Use your phone to explore 3D habitats and interact with wildlife through spatial computing.

Interactive Conservation Pledges: Users can make commitments to environmental action and see their impact visualized in real-time through data-driven experiences.

Technology Stack

Unity 3D
Meta Quest SDK
ARKit / ARCore
Spatial Audio
Photogrammetry
WebXR

Impact & Results

The Our Planet XR experience has reached millions of users worldwide and received critical acclaim for its innovative approach to conservation storytelling:

  • 3M+ downloads across VR and AR platforms
  • 500K+ conservation pledges made through the experience
  • Featured at Tribeca Film Festival and SXSW
  • Webby Award nominee for Best VR Experience
  • Netflix Editor's Choice recognition
